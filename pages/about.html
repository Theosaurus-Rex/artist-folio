Theo Harris is a Fine Artist from Sydney, Australia.

Inspired by Australian birds and his experiences as a transgender man, Theo creates works in dry media focusing on themes of identity, aesthetics, and inner conflict.

In November 2019, Theo was nominated as a finalist in the prestigious Fisher's Ghost Art Award for his drawing "Blue Brilliance", which exhibited at the Campbelltown Arts Centre.

In April 2021, his work will be shown in his first solo show, "An Ornithologist's Guide to People-Watching", at gaffa gallery, Sydney NSW.
